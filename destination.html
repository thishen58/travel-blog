Lakshapana, Sri Lanka

Lakshapana is one of the most beautiful and highest waterfalls in Sri Lanka, located in the central hills. Surrounded by lush greenery, it offers an incredible scenic experience, especially for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Highlights/Activities

  • Trek to the base of the waterfall
  • Photography of the cascading waters and surrounding views
  • Explore the nearby tea plantations
  • Enjoy a peaceful nature walk
